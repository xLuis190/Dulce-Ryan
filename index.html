Dulce And Ryan

Our Story

April 2020

¿How we met?

Ryan and Dulce met in April 2020, and as many couples do now days texting and video call was the best way to communicate. One day Ryan invited Dulce on a dinner date and this is how this story began, between laughs and adventures they got to know each other better, the language was not an obstacle for them.

July 2020
Ryan's Family

In July Ryan introduced Dulce to his parents for independence day celebration, started to have some fun together, weekend getaway with Ryan’s sisters and without noticing they become best friends. Mean while Ryan was planning a big surprise for Dulce that will change the relationship. To make his plan happen he needs some help, so his family jumped in to support him. So during a family dinner they gathered together to get the answers that Ryan needed.

November 2020
Thanksgiving

During thanksgiving time Dulce’s sisters traveled to Houston to visit her and meet Ryan for the first time, they all enjoyed the weekend in Texas style.

December 2020

Dulce's Family

Christmas time had come and Dulce’s family was planning to spend the holidays in New York. Ryan and Dulce decided to join the plan and celebrate in the Big Apple, visiting the Statue of Liberty, Empire State, Christmas dinner at Dulce’s sisters was a must for the trip. Ryan enjoyed the time with her family and got the courage to ask her father for her hand in marriage.

April 2021

The proposal

Once he got his approval he was missing the most important Yes! Dulce’s. So Ryan decided to include her family in his plans and together they planed a trip to Jamaica Beach, Texas. With a photo shoot as an excuse, Ryan convince her to travel to Galveston where all our families were reunited to celebrate. While the happy couple was at the photo session, all family came out for a Big Surprise! Dulce cannot believe what she saw, all her family together including her brother who she hasn’t seen over a year was there. Dulce is excited and Ryan was preparing to ask the question. “Come here I love you! with all my heart, you mean the world to me, will you marry me?” and there he was, on his knee, holding the ring. Then Dulce say Yes! We all spent the weekend at the beach house.
THE END… our history has just begun

Ryan

He was born in San Diego California but when he was 4 years old they moved to Angelton Texas. He is an incredible person, he always thinks a lot about others and he loves to make Dulce Happy, he always looks for a way to surprise her.

Dulce

She was born in Zapopan Jalisco, when she was 26 years old she moved to Houston, TX as an Au Pair, she loves spending time with children and pets. She has a big heart and likes to help people.
